Got Something to Share?

This is your chance. We accept a wide range of digital projects: videos, code repositories, graphic designs, PDFs of your research, animations, music, and more. If you made it, we want to see it.

Submission Guidelines

  • You must be between 13-19 years old and of Nepali origin.
  • Your work must be your own. Collaborations are welcome, but give credit!
  • Be respectful. No hate speech or harmful content.
  • Provide a link to your project (YouTube, GitHub, Google Drive, Figma, etc.).

Ready? Let's Go.

We use a Google Form to handle submissions securely. It allows you to upload files and provide all the necessary details. Our team will review every submission and feature the best ones on the Discover page.
