Meet the Founders

NepalNext is driven by a unique partnership that bridges professional experience with the voice of the next generation.

Zachary Lewis

Co-Founder & Technical Lead

As a full-time software engineer, Zachary provides the architectural backbone for NepalNext. His professional expertise is matched by a deep-seated passion for civic action and community leadership. A fierce advocate for empowering vulnerable populations, Zachary has extensive experience working with marginalized youth, supporting children with special needs, and leading drug prevention initiatives. This blend of technical skill and grassroots dedication ensures NepalNext is not only a robust platform but also one with a strong, ethical, and community-focused mission.
