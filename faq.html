Frequently Asked Questions

Who can submit an idea?

We welcome submissions from all young people aged 13 to 19 who are passionate about the future of Nepal.

What kind of ideas can I submit?

You can submit your ideas in a variety of formats, including videos, PDFs, documents, slideshows, and more. We encourage creativity and welcome all well-researched and thoughtful proposals.

What is the ultimate goal of my submission?

Our primary goal is to provide a powerful platform for your ideas to be heard by those who can make a difference. We aim to connect your innovative solutions with leaders in Nepal's government and corporate sectors, giving your ideas a real chance to help shape the nation's future.

What happens to my submission after I send it?

After you submit your idea, our team will review it. Exceptional submissions may be featured on our website's "Discover" page, included in the NepalNext manifesto, or presented directly to influential leaders and organizations. We are committed to giving your ideas the visibility and consideration they deserve.
