Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? We've answered the most common ones below. If you can't find your answer, please feel free to contact us.

For Creators

Who is eligible to submit a project?

Submissions are open to anyone aged 13-19 of Nepali origin, regardless of where you currently live. We welcome both individuals and teams.

What kind of projects can I submit?

Almost anything you can share digitally! We are looking for creativity and innovation in any field. This includes:

  • Tech & Code: Apps, websites, games, GitHub repositories.
  • Research & Science: PDFs of research papers, science project proposals, environmental solutions.
  • Arts & Media: Short films (YouTube/Vimeo links), animations, graphic designs, digital art, music.
  • Social Impact: Proposals for community projects, presentations, awareness campaigns.
What happens after I submit my project?

Your submission follows a two-pathway process:

1. Showcase: All approved projects are featured on our Discover page for public visibility.

2. Catalyst: If your project has a practical, real-world application, our team will review it for potential collaboration. We may then reach out to connect you with relevant organizations, mentors, or authorities to explore how to take your idea further.

Do I lose the rights to my idea if I submit it?

No, absolutely not. You retain 100% ownership and intellectual property rights to your work. By submitting, you only grant us permission to display it on our website and to connect you with potential partners on your behalf.

Is there any cost to submit or be featured?

No. NepalNext is completely free for creators. Our mission is to support and amplify you, not to profit from you.

For Partners & Supporters

How can my organization get involved?

We are always looking for partners who can help turn these ideas into reality. You can get involved by:

  • Providing mentorship to young creators.
  • Offering resources or funding for promising pilot projects.
  • Collaborating with us to review projects in your field of expertise.

Please get in touch via our Contact Page to start a conversation.

How do you ensure the quality of projects?

Our team, led by our co-founders, reviews every submission to ensure it meets our guidelines: it must be original, respectful, and demonstrate genuine effort and creativity. For projects we consider for the "Catalyst" pathway, we conduct a more thorough review of their feasibility and potential impact.
